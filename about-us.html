Founder And CEO​​

K. Veenanath, a Master of Design and Construction Management, founded Anagha Agaja Built Construction Company with a vision to revolutionize the Indian construction industry. Anagha Agaja Built Construction Company has gained recognition for its exceptional craftsmanship and innovative construction. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to delivering excellence, we transform spaces into beautiful and functional environments that leave a lasting impression.​​
